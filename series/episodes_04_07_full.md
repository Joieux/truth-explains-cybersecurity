This document fully maps episodes 04 through 07.

Episode 04
Title
How hackers really think and why beginners misunderstand it

Purpose
Replace movie myths with real mental models.
Help beginners understand that most attacks are about opportunity and people.

Core truth
Most attacks are not genius.
They are repetition, automation, and taking advantage of normal human behavior.

Outline
Opening
Come sit with me.
Let us clear up the biggest misunderstanding.
Most hacking is not magic. It is leverage.

Point 1
Attackers look for the easiest path
They choose targets with low effort and high payoff
They reuse what works

Visual notes
Simple path diagram
Easy path highlighted

Point 2
People are part of the system
Phishing works because humans are busy
Social engineering is often more effective than code

Visual notes
Inbox visuals
Login page visuals

Point 3
Most attacks follow a pattern
Find an opening
Get access
Expand access
Stay hidden
Cash out or cause damage

Visual notes
Five step timeline
Minimal icons

Point 4
Beginner mistake
Thinking you must learn everything
Real skill is learning how to ask what could go wrong

Visual notes
Question on screen
What could go wrong here

Soft close
You do not need to become paranoid.
You need to become observant.
Next video we will talk about why foundations beat shortcuts.
