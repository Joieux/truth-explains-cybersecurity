# Truth Explains Shorts template

Goal
One calm, clear idea in under 45 seconds.
No hype. No fear. No rushing.

Target length
25 to 45 seconds

On screen style
Big readable captions
One thought per line
Simple background visuals
Slow cuts

Structure

0:00 to 0:03
Open the door
Say something that makes a beginner feel seen.

Voice lines
Come sit with me for a second.
If cybersecurity feels confusing, you are not behind.

Visual note
Text on screen: You are not behind

0:03 to 0:10
Name the concept
Define it like an auntie would.

Voice lines
Here is what people mean when they say: [concept].
It simply means: [plain meaning].

Visual note
Text on screen: [concept] equals [plain meaning]

0:10 to 0:30
One example
Make it real with a simple everyday scenario.

Voice lines
For example: [scenario].
If that happens, the risk is: [risk].
The smart move is: [one practical habit].

Visual note
Show simple icons or stock footage that matches scenario

0:30 to 0:40
Truth line
The one sentence they remember.

Voice lines
The truth is: [truth line].

Visual note
Text on screen: The truth is: [truth line]

0:40 to 0:45
Soft close
Invite them back without pressure.

Voice lines
If you want calm explanations like this, follow Truth Explains.
I will be right here.

Visual note
Channel name on screen: Truth Explains
Tagline on screen: Cybersecurity explained by Truth, with patience

Caption rules
Short lines
No jargon without a quick definition
No exclamation points
Let sentences end cleanly
