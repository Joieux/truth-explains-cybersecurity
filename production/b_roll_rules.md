# B roll rules

Rule zero
If a visual distracts, remove it.

## What to use

Calm desk scenes
Notebooks and pens
Hands using a phone or laptop
Servers and infrastructure
Simple data flow visuals
City infrastructure
Abstract tech motion backgrounds

## What to avoid

Fast cuts
Glitch effects
Overused cyber tropes
Busy scenes with lots of movement
Anything that feels like a movie trailer

## Edit pacing

Audio first.
Then visuals under it.
Do not cut visuals mid sentence.
Let ideas land.
