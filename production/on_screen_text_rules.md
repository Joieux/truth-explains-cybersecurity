# On screen text rules

Purpose
Support understanding.
Never decorate.
Never compete with the voice.

## Rules

One idea per screen.
No scrolling text.
No bouncing animations.
Use fade in and fade out only.

Keep text large and readable.
Short lines only.

## When to use text

Defining a term
Highlighting a truth line
Reinforcing reassurance

## Examples

The core question
What could go wrong here
You are allowed to move slowly
Truth over hype

## Timing

Let the voice finish the sentence.
Then bring the text on screen.
Hold it long enough to read comfortably.
Then fade out.
