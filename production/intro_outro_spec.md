# Intro and outro spec

## Intro

Length
1 to 2 seconds

On screen text
Truth Explains
Cybersecurity explained by Truth, with patience.

Motion
Fade in, fade out

Rule
Go straight into the opening line.
No extra intro sequence.

## Outro

Length
3 to 5 seconds

On screen text
Truth Explains
Cybersecurity explained by Truth, with patience.

Motion
Fade in, fade out

Rule
No music swell.
No loud subscribe animation.
