# Truth Explains publishing checklist

Use this checklist before marking a video as complete.

## Script

- Script follows channel voice and tone
- Line breaks added for AI pacing
- Acronyms spelled out on first use
- Closing includes reassurance

## Voice

- Correct ElevenLabs preset used
- Opening minute sounds grounded
- Pacing is calm and unforced
- No noticeable artifacts or glitches

## Visuals

- Audio placed first on timeline
- Visuals do not rush sentences
- No distracting motion
- Channel name and tagline used once only

## Music

- Ambient or minimal track
- No vocals
- Music barely noticeable
- No dramatic changes or swells

## Upload details

- Title is clear and honest
- Description explains who this is for
- Pinned comment added
- End screen is simple
- Thumbnail readable on phone

## Final check

- Watched once without editing
- Nothing feels rushed
- Nothing feels performative
- Video feels like a conversation

If everything above is true, publish and step away.
