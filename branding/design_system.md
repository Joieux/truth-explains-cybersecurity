# Truth Explains design system

Channel name
Truth Explains

Tagline
Cybersecurity explained by Truth, with patience.

Design goal
Quiet confidence.
Warm.
Grounded.
Nothing flashy.
The voice leads.
The visuals support.

## Visual identity

Overall feel
Calm and grown.
Clear and readable on a phone.
No trend chasing.

Avoid
Hacker hoodie imagery
Green code rain
Glitch effects
Over animated text

Prefer
Warm neutral backgrounds
Simple motion
Human scale visuals like desks, notebooks, hands, everyday technology

## Color palette

Primary backgrounds
Warm charcoal
Deep brown

Primary text
Soft cream
Off white

Optional accent
Muted gold
Dusty terracotta

Rule
Use accent sparingly.
If a color looks trendy, do not use it.

## Typography

Use one clean sans serif font only.
Good options
Inter
Montserrat
Source Sans Pro

Weights
Regular
Semi bold

Rules
No thin fonts
No extra bold
No decorative fonts

## Motion rules

Keep motion slow.
Fade in and fade out.
No bouncing.
No shaking.
No glitch.

Intro
No animation longer than 2 seconds.
Channel name and tagline only.
Then straight into content.

Outro
Fade to channel name and tagline.
No music swell.
No loud subscribe animation.

## Layout rules

Whitespace is your friend.
Do not cram the screen.
One idea per screen.
Large readable text.

Consistency beats creativity.
Repeat the same layout patterns across videos.
