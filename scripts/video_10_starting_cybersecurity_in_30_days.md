title: Starting cybersecurity in 30 days
episode: 10

script
Welcome.

I’m Auntie Truth,

and we’re going to make cybersecurity
make sense.

Today we are talking about getting started in cybersecurity.

Because a lot of people want in,
but they think they have to be a genius first.

You do not.

You need a plan.

Here is a simple 30 day path.

Week one,
learn the basics.

What is phishing.
What is malware.
What is two factor.
What is a firewall.

Keep it simple.

Week two,
learn how the internet works.

IP address.
DNS.
HTTP and HTTPS.

Not to memorize,
to understand what you are looking at.

Week three,
practice on purpose.

Do beginner labs.
Capture the flag beginner rooms.
Learn by doing.

Week four,
build something small.

Write a one page guide.
Make a home security checklist.
Document what you learned.
Post a project.

That is how you show progress.

Now the most important part.

Be consistent.

Fifteen minutes a day beats
three hours once a month.

Action step.

Pick one learning track
and do fifteen minutes today.

Do not wait for motivation.

We are building momentum.

If this helped you,
stay with me.

We’re learning how to move smarter online,
not trying to be perfect.

And if this felt useful,
liking, sharing, or subscribing
helps it reach more people
who need it.
