## About this project

Truth Explains is an educational YouTube channel focused on explaining cybersecurity concepts slowly, clearly, and without hype.

This project is designed for beginners, career switchers, and curious learners who want understanding before tools.

The goal is clarity, not performance.
The tone is calm, grounded, and honest.

## Channel philosophy

Cybersecurity is not about memorizing tools or proving intelligence.
It is about understanding systems, people, and risk.

This channel prioritizes:
- strong foundations
- clear thinking
- patience over urgency
- truth over hype

## Repository structure

This repository contains scripts, branding, production guidelines, and voice settings used to produce the Truth Explains channel.

Scripts are written for calm AI narration and minimal visuals.
All content is versioned to support long term growth.

## AI voice approach

The channel uses a calm female AI voice with low exaggeration and slow pacing.
Voice settings and formatting rules are documented in the voice folder.
